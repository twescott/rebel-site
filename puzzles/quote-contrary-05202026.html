Rebel’s Puzzles

Rebel’s Puzzles is a social experiment being conducted by Rebel’s Dad on the patrons of Growlerz Trivia Night. He thinks this will be a fun addition to Caryn’s awesome trivia questions. Like Rebel, Rebel’s Dad responds well to feedback. Train him well.

Each Sunday, there will be a new puzzle on Rebel’s Blog, followed by a new “Find It!” puzzle on Tuesday. These puzzles will provide hints to the weekly Growlerz Trivia Night puzzle.

Tonight’s Puzzle

Tonight’s puzzle is called “Quote Contrary”. You will be presented with groups of four words, three of which have something in common. You will get points for correctly identifying the commonality for each group. Rearrange the words that don’t belong to form a famous quote. You’ll get 1 point for each correct word. Additional points can be obtained by correctly identifying the source of the quote.

Example:

In each group, find the word that doesn’t belong. The contrary words spell out a well-known quote.
